Privacy Policy for Dadi Flight App

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Dadi Flight App is designed to play a scheduled spiritual audio experience. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple: we do not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Information We Collect

Dadi Flight App does not collect personal information from users. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or payment information, and does not use advertising trackers.

Internet Access

The app may connect to the internet to fetch journey timing information used to synchronize audio playback. This request is used only for app functionality and does not include personal user information.

Local Storage

The app may store basic playback timing information locally on your device so the experience can continue correctly. This local information is not sent to us.

Audio Playback

The app uses audio playback features, including background audio, so the scheduled audio can continue when the screen is locked or the app is in the background.

Third-Party Services

Dadi Flight App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app's functionality changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the project support page: https://github.com/Adarshtulsyan/Dadi-Flight-iOS/issues
